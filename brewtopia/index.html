Brewtopia

Branding & Landing Page Concept

Objective

1. Design a full brand identity for a local coffee shop effectively capturing & comunicating their identity, values & personality.

2. Design a landing page in line with new brand identity, advertising the grand opening of the coffee shop & motivating customers to enagage with the brand & attend opening weekend.

The Problem

A local coffee shop were getting ready to lanch their new business. They have a great location in a popular neighbourhood but needed help creating a brand identity that reflected their products, values & personality & which sucessfully resonated with the local community.

They also planned to hold a Grand Opening Weekend & needed help creating a landing page to advertise the event & which met the folowing goals: - attract as many customers as possible to the coffee shop during the opening week end. - help to form long-term relationship with potential customers.

The Solution

To design a full brand identity package including colour scheme, typography, logo, brand imagery & icons, social media headers & a brand style guide.

Design & build a landing page to advertise the Grand Opening weekend of the coffee shop & which successfully encourage the local community to attend through.

Design, Planning & Research

After defining the problem and identifying the solution, the next step involves design, planning and research. In this phase, I built an inspirational mood board, sketched out ideas and designed brand elements & wireframes around user requirements.
